Main Points

  • No time to play long sessions of video games;
  • Plays arcade and puzzle games during commutes and meetings;
Goals

Goals are the reasons users perform tasks, not the tasks.

  • Practical Goals: Occupy some time
  • Personal Goals: Get a high score
Frustrations and Pain Points
  • Long chains of menus;
  • Distracting elements and sounds;
  • Unpausable games
Detailed Description

John is an IT specialist for a company. He enjoys playing videogames but due to long commutes and an increased amount of meeting due to a new project he has little to no time for them. So he found himself playing arcade and puzzle games on his phone during commutes and on his laptop during long meeting.


He finds it frustrating to navigate trough menus, to set thing up, especially when he wants to play something quick. And he also finds games that have elements to distract him, from both the game and the outside world, a pain to play.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Daily Commute


While on the bus toward work John pulls his phone from his pocket and opens Tetris. The app opens to the main menu, he clicks play and starts playing instantly. When he reaches game over he hits try again and plays for a few more rounds until he reaches his station.



Scenario 2: Meeting


During an office meeting, after the presented his progress report, John opens Tetris, clicks play and plays a few rounds while keeping an ear out if he has to answer any questions. Stopping the game when he has to answer and resuming playing afterwards.

Other Details

He likes boxes and stacking them up efficiently.

Background

  • Gender: Male
  • Location: London, England
  • Work place: CodeLife, CEO
  • School: Palo Alto High School (if required)
  • Technology Level:(if required)
Main Points

Alex possesses extensive experience and expertise in playing Tetris. Their prior participation in competitions and events showcases their dedication and skill in this specific game.

He also has a deep understanding of Tetris, including advanced strategies and techniques. Their proficiency sets them apart from casual players and contributes to their success in competitive gaming.

Alex desires a user interface in Tetris that is intuitive, streamlined, and optimized for performance.

He appreciates interfaces that facilitate smooth gameplay and enable them to achieve optimal results.

Goals

To feel challenged and stimulated by the Tetris game, avoiding feelings of boredom or underestimation.

To have fun and enjoy the Tetris gaming experience.

To achieve remarkable performance in Tetris, becoming recognized as a professional player.

To inspire and encourage other passionate gamers to engage in the Tetris gaming community.

Frustrations and Pain Points

Some of the difficulties the user has with the product.

  • Distracting visual effects;
  • Intrusive notifications;
  • Lack of customization options;
Detailed Description

Alex is a dedicated pro gamer who focuses on playing Tetris. He has been passionate about video games from a young age and has gained significant expertise in the field, particularly in Tetris. The game constitutes a major part of his life, and he aims to achieve high levels of skill and performance in this domain.


Persona Type: Supplemental (Pro gamer who plays Tetris)


Alex balances his gaming career with his professional life. He is an entrepreneur and owns his own technology-related business. This provides him with flexibility and autonomy in his work schedule, allowing him to dedicate time to his passion for video games.


Alex sought a simple and efficient interface during gameplay. They knew that an intuitive interface could make a significant difference in achieving optimal results. Alex desired smooth navigation, clear visual cues, and responsive controls that would enable them to execute moves swiftly and flawlessly.


Alex plays Tetris regularly, dedicating a significant number of hours per week to the game and practice to improve his skills..

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Daily Training Session

Alex uses the Tetris product during their daily training sessions.
They play on their gaming console, using a high-resolution monitor and a gamepad.
Alex's goal is to improve their speed and accuracy in clearing lines.
Frustration & Pain Point: Lack of real-time feedback on their performance.
End Point: Alex completes a challenging training session, achieving a new personal best score.

Scenario 2: Competitive Tournament

Alex participates in a local Tetris tournament held at a gaming convention.
They compete against skilled opponents on a stage with large screens for spectators.
Alex's goal is to win the tournament.
Frustration & Pain Point: Difficulty in adapting to different game setups and controls.
End Point: Alex emerges as the tournament champion.

Scenario 3: Casual Gaming with Friends

Alex invites friends over for a casual Tetris gaming session.
They play on a large TV screen using multiple wireless controllers.
Alex's goal is to have fun and enjoy a friendly competition with friends.
Frustration & Pain Point: Difficulty in customizing gameplay settings to accommodate different skill levels.
End Point: Alex and their friends engage in laughter and friendly banter, enjoying an entertaining gaming session together.
